Frequently Asked Questions

How does spaced repetition work?
After reviewing a card, you rate it as Again, Hard, Good, or Easy. KarteiDeck uses the SM-2 algorithm to schedule when you'll see that card again — cards you know well come back less often, cards you struggle with come back sooner.
How do I earn tokens?
Tokens are earned by reviewing cards. You earn more tokens when cards have longer intervals — meaning the SRS confirms you genuinely learned the word. There's a daily cap of 150 tokens to prevent gaming.
How do I unlock new tiers?
Each tier unlocks when you reach Strong or Mastered status on enough words in the current tier. Tier 1 requires 200 words, Tier 2 requires 300, and so on.
Can I change the number of cards per session?
Yes — go to Profile → Settings and adjust the sliders for new cards and review cards per session.
How do I reset my progress?
Go to Profile → Settings → scroll down to Data → tap "Reset all progress". This will reset all your cards, XP, streaks, and tokens.
Is my data backed up?
Your data is stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, your progress will be lost. iCloud sync is planned for a future update.
How do I unlock banners and cosmetics?
Go to Profile → Banners or Profile → Cosmetics. Some items are free, others cost tokens which you earn by studying.
Why can't I start another session today?
KarteiDeck limits new cards per day to protect your memory consolidation. You can adjust this limit in Settings. Due cards are always available to review.
